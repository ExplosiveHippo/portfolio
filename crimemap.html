ROC Crime Map

Description: The ROC Crime Map is a personal project I created and worked primarily on at the Rochester Random Hacks of Kindness Hackathon. The application reads in a feed of violent crime reports in Rochester. The purpose of the application is to allow people who may be unfamiliar with an area of the city to make safe decisions. The user can search for a specific address and get an idea of the crime activity in that area.

Tech: The Map is powered by Leaflet.js and utilized many community created plugins. The data is read from a JSON file and is used to grab a snapshot of the location with the Google Streetview API.
