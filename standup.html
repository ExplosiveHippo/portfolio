Standup Guys Landing Page

Description: This was a freelance project of mine for the Stand Up Guys non-profit organization in Rochester, NY. Stand Up Guy's goal is to educate men about domestic abuse. They needed a landing page to establish their more masculine rebranding and to serve as a gateway for men to continue to learn about the organization while the full site was developed.

Tech: The design was created in Photoshop and the site was developed using HTML and CSS and utilizes Google Web Fonts
