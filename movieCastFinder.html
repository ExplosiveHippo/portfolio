Movie Cast Finder

Description: As a big movie buff I've constantly run into the problem of vaguely recognizing a minor character in a movie from another film. Rather than digging through a gaint list on IMDB to find the actor, I developed Movie Cast Finder. All you have to do is enter the film you're watching and the film you remember the actor was in and you'll be returned a list of any actors that were in both films.

Tech: Movie Cast Finder is writen in jQuery and uses The Movie DB API.
