Dominion.js

Description: Dominion.js was my final project for Programming 4 for New Media. The starting cards are randomized every game so each game is unique. There is also an A.I system in place behind the scene. To see this you'll need to open a console. After each turn the A.I will complete it's own turn. After a game finishes a player can choose to enter their name and it will keep track of the players top 10 scores.

Tech: Object-Oriented Javascript, jQuery, PHP, Ajax and XML.
